Leading the technology ecosystem for Creative Group—end-to-end across the whole group.

One of my proudest professional achievements is leading and managing the complete technology ecosystem for Creative Group, a large business group in the manpower industry. I still maintain it myself—production care, ongoing updates, and day-by-day scale as the group grows.

For the past 3+ years, I have been solely responsible for building, scaling, and maintaining their entire digital and operational infrastructure. This includes enterprise CRM development, internal management software, operational workflow automation, AI-driven business process automation, lead generation systems, website development, Google Sheets automation, and end-to-end technology strategy across the organization.

I designed and developed a full-scale internal CRM and operations management platform from the ground up, which now powers the daily operations of 400–500+ employees across multiple departments, including manpower operations, accounts, passport processing, approvals, employee workflows, and other mission-critical business functions.

The system was architected using scalable engineering principles such as microservices, distributed systems, Redis caching, and event-driven architecture to ensure long-term performance, reliability, and business continuity.

What makes this achievement especially meaningful is that the entire system was built and is still maintained solely by me as a single engineer, from the earliest Google Sheets automations to advanced AI-powered operational systems, with ongoing updates and day-by-day scaling as real workloads grow. I transformed manual business processes into a centralized, scalable, and efficient digital ecosystem, and that work is still going on.

This project represents one of the biggest milestones of my career—not only because of its technical scale, but because it directly supports the core operations of an entire business group and has earned strong trust and recognition from the Managing Director, Chairman, and leadership team.
